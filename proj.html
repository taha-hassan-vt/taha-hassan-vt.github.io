Exploring dynamic dependencies for collaborative set theory visualizations

Developed a small-group collaborative visualization tool for set theoretic ideas. Evaluated algorithms to structure effective small-group collaboration, to assess user engagement in groups and recommend instructor intervention.


Predictive modeling of residential energy consumption signatures

Developed a recommender systems view of household load prediction (kernelized collaborative filtering). Useful for consumer targeting, feedback and demand response monetization.


Optimal energy management in smart homes

Design of an embedded energy management solution that integrated with renewable sources in grid-connected mode, minimized rolling blackouts and reduced electricity bill for the end-user.


Question answering in academic analytics

Design/implementation of a recommender system for suggesting appropriate graduate schools and research groups based on applicant credentials, spatial and temporal odds of admission, and research activity of target groups.


Manual-setup residential energy disaggregation

Worked on neural ensemble algorithms for non-invasively determining the number and types of appliance profiles from aggregate energy consumption of a household.
