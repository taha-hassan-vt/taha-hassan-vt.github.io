About Me


I'm a PhD student with the Department of Computer Science, Virginia Tech. I'm advised by Dr. Scott McCrickard. I work on statistical learning for trust-aware group recommendation (deep multi-task learning, Bayesian ensemble models), and bias and trust issues in recommendation UX (power and editorial authority imbalance, fairness preservation, filter bubbles, distributive justice). My interests are motivated by real-world problems in education, sustainability and behavioral finance.

Email me at taha@vt.edu.
